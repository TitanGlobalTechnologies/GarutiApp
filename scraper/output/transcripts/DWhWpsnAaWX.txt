We recently posted that we had several buyers looking for multi-family real estate on Long Island. Some of the hardest real estate to come across. Within three days of making that post, we had over tens interested sellers reach out to us, saying that they own a multi-family home and they would be willing to sell it. It just goes to show you how powerful social media can be when you stick to it. We've been posting consistently for over one year now. And even though the results don't come right away, when they do come, they severely start to compound. So now whenever we need something or looking for a specific connection, all we have to do is post a video and boom, our inbox starts flooding with messages. Social media sucks in the beginning and it is not easy, but it is the best investment to make in your real estate career.
