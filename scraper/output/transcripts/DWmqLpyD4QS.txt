If you had told me I would be presenting one of Port Royals' most iconic bay front estates, I might have had to pause for a moment and take it in because homes like this do not come along often. Set on 268 feet of Naples Bay Frontage, 1975 Gallyan Drive is the kind of property that feels less like a listing and more like a legacy. Sitting over 11,000 square feet of living space with six bedrooms, eight baths, and water views that will follow you from room to room. Inside, every space feels designed for a life beautifully lived and beautifully hosted from the sweeping staircase to the theater, but the real showstopper is outside. A disappearing edge pool meeting the horizon of Naples Bay, and a 93-foot dock with direct golf access. Because in Port Royals, the two luxury is not just the home, it's the lifestyle that surrounds it. This is 1975 Gallyan Drive, and it's extraordinary.
