Everywhere you look in Lehigh, something new is popping up. Along Leigh Boulevard, a brand new shopping center is bringing in the kind of retail and dining our community has been waiting for, with room for restaurants, coffee shops, and even big grocery store chains. And right here, off of Gunnery Road and Daniel's Parkway, thousands of new homes and over 350,000 square feet of commercial space are on the way. From Stade Road 82 to Homestead Road to Leigh Boulevard, every major road is being surrounded by new development. The future of Lehigh is happening right now. The question is, will you be a part of it?
