Okay so this actually happened and it's wild. A homeowner here in Southwest Florida was stuck. Couldn't sell. Traditional listings were going nowhere and price cuts weren't helping either.

So instead of panicking he got creative. He listed the house for ten dollars. Not the sale price. An entry fee for a contest. People paid ten bucks for a shot at winning the home outright.

Nearly 200,000 people entered. He walked away with around two million dollars. Way more than any standard listing would have gotten him in this market.

And the winner got a free house in Florida. Life changing for both of them.

Look the Fort Myers market has slowed down for some sellers right now. Inventory is up and buyers have options. But creative marketing still cuts through all of that noise.

So I gotta ask you this. If your home wasn't selling in Fort Myers today, would you try something this unconventional to get it done?
