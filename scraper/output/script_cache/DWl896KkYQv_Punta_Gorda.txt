Real talk about Punta Gorda real estate that nobody wants to say out loud.

Back in 2019 the average home here was around $265k and you needed roughly $66k household income to make it work comfortably. Fast forward to today and homes in Punta Gorda are sitting closer to $400k plus. With property taxes climbing, insurance going through the roof, and mortgage rates still high, you now need closer to $100k household income to stay at a healthy debt to income ratio.

Most people are not there yet and that is totally okay.

The good news is the market is leveling off. My listings that are actually moving right now are priced at 2020 to 2022 numbers. That means buyers finally have real negotiating power in Punta Gorda for the first time in years.

Homes are long term wealth builders not get rich quick flips. Always have been.
