2026 is going to be a BIG year for Cape Coral real estate and here are the three things I'm watching closely

First, interest rates. They jumped from 3% all the way to nearly 8% back in 2023. Since then they've been slowly dropping and there's real hope they keep coming down through 2026. That's huge for our market here on the Cape.

Second, first time buyers. The average age jumped from 28 in 1991 to 40 today. As rates get friendlier, younger buyers could finally break into Cape Coral's waterfront and canal home market.

Third, the mortgage lock-in effect. Tons of Cape Coral homeowners are sitting on 3% rates and feel stuck. But life doesn't pause for interest rates. Job changes, divorce, growing families, those things happen regardless.

When rates drop more, expect Cape Coral inventory to finally open up.
