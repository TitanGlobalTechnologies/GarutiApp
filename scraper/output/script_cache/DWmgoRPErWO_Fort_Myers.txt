Here's the truth about investing in Fort Myers real estate that nobody wants to hear...

Smart investors build a buy box. They pick specific zip codes like 33901 or 33919 and stick to them. They run their own comps using actual MLS data.

Rookie investors? They chase whatever looks good on Zillow. They trust the Zestimate like it's gospel. They fall in love with a house in Cape Coral or Gateway and try to force numbers that just don't work.

Fort Myers is genuinely one of the best markets in Florida right now. But that doesn't mean every deal is a good deal.

The investors winning here are disciplined. If the numbers don't work, they move on. No emotions. No exceptions.

The ones losing money are married to properties instead of profits.

So be honest with me... are you currently investing based on strategy or based on feelings?
