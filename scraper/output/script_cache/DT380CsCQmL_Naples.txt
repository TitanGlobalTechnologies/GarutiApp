Here in Naples a seller couldn't move their home no matter what they tried. Price drops weren't working. Showings were slow. So instead of panicking they got creative.

They listed the home for ten dollars. Not to sell it cheap but to run a contest. People paid ten bucks to enter and the idea went absolutely viral. Nearly 200,000 people jumped in.

The final result? Around two million dollars in entries. Way more than the original asking price. And someone walked away with a gorgeous Naples home as a life changing prize.

Look the Naples market is competitive right now and sometimes the old playbook just doesn't cut it. The sellers who win are thinking outside the box completely.

Attention is the new price reduction. Creative marketing can do what traditional listings sometimes can't.
