Okay so this story is wild and I had to share it with you guys. A homeowner was struggling to sell their place, sound familiar in today's Cape Coral market? Instead of dropping the price again, they did something completely different. They advertised the home for ten dollars.

It was actually a raffle style contest. People paid ten dollars to enter, nearly two hundred thousand people joined, and by the end the homeowner walked away with roughly two million dollars. Way more than any traditional listing wouldve gotten them.

Now I'm not saying this is the move for your Cape Coral home but the lesson here is huge. Creative marketing beats price cuts almost every single time. Whether you're on the waterfront or in a Cape Coral canal community, how you present your home matters more than people think.
