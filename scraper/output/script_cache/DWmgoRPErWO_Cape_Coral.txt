When starting out investing in Cape Coral real estate, don't be the one who makes these mistakes.

Smart move: build a buy box and stick to it. Maybe that's waterfront canal homes, or properties near the Coral Oaks area. Pick your lane.

Dumb move: chasing every shiny listing in every zip code from 33904 to 33993.

Smart move: run your own comps using MLS data for Cape Coral specifically. This market is unique.

Dumb move: trusting the Zestimate on a Gulf access canal home. It will lie to you.

Smart move: if the numbers don't work in Cape Coral's current market, you move on. Period.

Dumb move: falling in love with the boat dock and the sunset views and forcing the deal anyway.

Cape Coral has incredible investment opportunities right now but only if you stay disciplined.
