Hey Fort Myers homebuyers and investors, real talk for a second.

Developers here are spending big on premium marketing, beautiful renderings, luxury branding. And honestly? It works. But here is what nobody talks about.

The moment you sign that contract, the communication gets weird. Emails pile up. Calls go unanswered. You are left wondering what is happening with your investment.

Fort Myers is booming right now. New construction all over Cape Coral, Gateway, Estero. Buyers are putting serious money down and then basically crossing their fingers for 12 to 24 months.

The builders who are winning long term are the ones keeping buyers engaged AFTER the sale. Real updates. Real transparency. Real relationship building.

Because a happy buyer in Fort Myers tells their neighbors, their coworkers, their whole Facebook group. A frustrated buyer does the same thing.

Your experience after closing matters just as much as the sale itself.

So here is my question for you. When you bought or invested in a new construction home, did the builder keep you in the loop or leave you guessing?
