Here's the truth about buying a home in Bonita Springs right now and nobody's really talking about it.

Back in 2019 a home here ran around $300,000. You needed roughly $70,000 household income to make the numbers work at a healthy debt to income ratio.

Fast forward to today. Bonita Springs median home prices are pushing $500,000 or higher depending on the community. Your mortgage payment alone could hit $3,500 a month. To stay financially comfortable you realistically need a household income closer to $120,000.

Most people aren't there yet and that is completely okay.

Here's what I'm actually seeing on the ground. My listings that are moving are priced at 2020 to 2022 levels. Buyers have options right now. The rental market is oversaturated. Prices are slowly correcting.

If you bought in 2021 or 2022 just hold on. Real estate in Bonita Springs is a long game not a flip.
