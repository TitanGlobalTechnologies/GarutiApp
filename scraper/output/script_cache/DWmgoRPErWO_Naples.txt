Starting out investing in Naples real estate? Don't be the dumb money in the room.

Smart investors pick a specific buy box and stick to it. Maybe that's condos under $500k in North Naples or single family homes near East Naples. They identify 2 or 3 zip codes and go deep.

Dumb money? They chase anything that looks pretty. A gorgeous Gulf access home that bleeds cash every month is still a bad deal.

Smart investors run their own comps using actual MLS data. Dumb money trusts the Zestimate in a market like Naples where prices shift block by block.

And the biggest one... smart investors walk away when the numbers don't work. Dumb money falls in love with the lanai and talks themselves into a bad investment.

Naples is an incredible market but it will humble you fast if you're emotional about it.
