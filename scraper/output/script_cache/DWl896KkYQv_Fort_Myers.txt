Real talk about Fort Myers home prices because this is eye opening.

Back in 2019 the average home here was around $265k and you needed roughly $66k household income to comfortably afford it. Fast forward to today and prices have climbed to the $400k range with mortgage payments pushing $3200 a month. To stay at a healthy debt to income ratio you realistically need about $108k household income now.

Most people in Fort Myers aren't there yet and that is totally okay.

Here's the good news though. The market is leveling off. A lot of what's actually selling right now in Cape Coral and Fort Myers is priced back at 2020 to 2022 levels. That's real opportunity for local buyers who got priced out before.

If you bought at peak pricing don't panic. Homes build wealth over years not months.

The market is shifting in buyers favor right now.
