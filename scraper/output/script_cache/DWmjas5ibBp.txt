Lost $15k on a deal in Naples and here is what actually happened.

Seller randomly terminated. Found out the next day she went under contract with MY buyer. Cut me out completely. And get this... she was a licensed realtor in New York. She knew EXACTLY what she was doing.

I was heated. Wanted to go scorched earth on her.

But instead I did something that made me way more money long term. I sat down, reviewed every single contract I had, tightened everything up, and built systems so no seller could EVER do that to me again.

That one painful loss turned into my strongest protection going forward.

The agents who eat in this market are not the ones who avoid failure. They are the ones who learn faster than everyone else.
