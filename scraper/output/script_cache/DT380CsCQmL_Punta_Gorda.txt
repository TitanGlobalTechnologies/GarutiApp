Okay so this story is wild and it actually made me think about the Punta Gorda market in a totally different way.

A homeowner was stuck. Months on the market, no serious buyers, kept dropping the price. Sound familiar? Instead of another price cut he listed the house for ten dollars. Not to sell it cheap but to run a contest. Ten bucks to enter.

Two hundred thousand people entered. He walked away with around two million dollars. Way more than his original asking price. Then he legally handed the keys to the winner.

Here in Punta Gorda where inventory moves fast but some properties still sit, creative marketing can completely change the game. Attention beats pricing every single time.

You do not have to give your home away just because it is not moving the traditional way.

So tell me, if your Punta Gorda home was sitting on the market right now, would you try something unconventional to sell it?
