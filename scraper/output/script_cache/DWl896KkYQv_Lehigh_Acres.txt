Here's the real talk about buying in Lehigh Acres right now.

Back in 2019, homes here were averaging around $180,000. The income needed to comfortably afford that was totally doable for most families in the area.

Fast forward to today and that same home is pushing $320,000. Your mortgage payment is somewhere around $2,400 to $2,600 a month. To keep a healthy debt to income ratio you realistically need a household income close to $85,000 or more.

Most Lehigh Acres families aren't there yet and that's completely okay.

What I'm actually seeing sell right now are homes priced at 2020 and 2021 levels. Sellers who get that are closing deals. Sellers holding onto peak pricing are just sitting.

The rental market here is also getting flooded. Competition is high. This is actually great news if you're a local buyer who's been waiting.
