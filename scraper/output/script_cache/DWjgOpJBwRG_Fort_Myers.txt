We are talking 3 bed 2.5 bath just under 2600 square feet. The whole property is fully fenced and there is a retaining wall around the home itself. Inside you get engineered hardwood floors, natural wood beams running from the living room straight into the kitchen, a reclaimed wood island with 52 inch custom cabinetry and built in chairs. It is stunning.

Outside you have a 4 car garage, an outdoor workshop, and another structure to do whatever you want with. And you are only minutes from downtown Fort Myers which honestly has so much more charm than people give it credit for.

Properties like this in Lee County do not sit long. What would you do with 7 acres in Fort Myers?
