Here's my honest take on Bonita Springs real estate in 2026...

The overall vibe? Positive but not explosive. The US economy is still growing 2 to 3% and we have serious capital flowing into Southwest Florida. Bonita Springs specifically keeps attracting buyers from the Northeast and Midwest who want out of cold winters and high taxes.

But here's the reality check. Rates are probably NOT dropping much. The national debt is sitting at $38 trillion with a trillion dollars a year just in interest payments. That's a massive anchor keeping mortgage rates elevated longer than most people want to admit.

For Bonita Springs that means the market moves but it does not moonshot. Sellers need realistic pricing. Buyers who wait for rate drops might be waiting a long time.

The good news is waterfront properties and communities near Barefoot Beach are still holding value incredibly well.
