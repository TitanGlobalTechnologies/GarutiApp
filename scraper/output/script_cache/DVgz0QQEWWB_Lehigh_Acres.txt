2026 is about to be a wild year for real estate here in Lehigh Acres and I'm watching three things closely.

First, interest rates. Back in 2022 they shot up from around 3% and hit nearly 8% by late 2023. Since then they've been slowly dropping and a lot of people think 2026 could bring even more relief for buyers here in Southwest Florida.

Second, first time buyers. In 1991 the average age was 28. Now it's 40. As prices stabilize in Lehigh Acres we could finally see younger buyers jumping in.

Third, the mortgage lock-in effect. Tons of Lehigh Acres homeowners are sitting on 3% rates and feel stuck. But here's the thing, life doesn't pause for interest rates. New jobs, divorces, growing families, people move regardless.

As rates drop we could see a flood of inventory hit our local market.
