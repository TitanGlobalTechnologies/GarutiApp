2026 is about to be a big deal for Naples real estate and here are the three things I am watching closely.

First, interest rates. They shot up from 3% all the way to nearly 8% back in 2023. Since then they have been slowly coming down and there is real hope we see more drops this year. That is huge for buyers eyeing Naples.

Second, first time buyers. The average age jumped from 28 in 1991 to 40 years old now. As Naples becomes more accessible, younger buyers could finally get their foot in the door here.

Third, the mortgage lock in effect. A lot of Naples homeowners are sitting on 3% rates and feel stuck. But life happens. New jobs, divorces, growing families. Those moments do not wait for perfect rates.
