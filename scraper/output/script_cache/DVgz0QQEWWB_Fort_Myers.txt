2026 is going to be a big year for Fort Myers real estate and here are the three things I am watching closely.

First, interest rates. Back in 2022 rates jumped from 3% and hit nearly 8% by late 2023. Since then they have been slowly dropping and there is real speculation we could see more relief in 2026.

Second, first time buyers. In 1991 the average first time buyer was 28. Today they are 40. If rates keep coming down here in Southwest Florida we could finally see younger buyers jumping into the market.

Third, the rate lock situation. So many homeowners are sitting on 3% mortgages and do not want to give that up. But here is the thing, life does not wait. Jobs, relationships, family changes, they keep happening regardless of rates.
