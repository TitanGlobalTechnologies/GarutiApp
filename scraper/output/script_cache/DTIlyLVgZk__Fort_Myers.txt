Everyone is watching interest rates going into 2026 but that is NOT the biggest change coming in Fort Myers right now.

What I'm actually seeing here is buyers coming back with real motivation. Like serious buyers who disappeared for the last couple years are suddenly active again.

Rates are slowly trending down and with new Fed leadership expected this May there is real optimism around more cuts coming.

On top of that inventory in Fort Myers and Southwest Florida is finally building back up. More choices. More balance. Less of that panic energy we had a few years ago.

If you are planning to buy in Cape Coral, Bonita Springs, or anywhere in Lee County this year or thinking about selling your Fort Myers home buckle up because this market is going to look completely different from anything we have seen in the last five years.

Are you waiting for rates to drop more or are you ready to move now?
