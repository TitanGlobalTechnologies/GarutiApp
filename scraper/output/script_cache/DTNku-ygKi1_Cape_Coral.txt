Here's what you need to know about Cape Coral real estate heading into 2026.

Inventory is climbing but so is buyer demand from wealthy out of state families looking for waterfront lifestyle and warm weather year round. We're talking multi generational homes with boat docks, pool setups, and enough space for the whole family to actually enjoy being together.

Cape Coral has over 400 miles of canals. That kind of scarcity does not exist everywhere. And that scarcity is exactly what keeps values strong even when other markets soften.

Prices in premium waterfront pockets of Cape Coral are still appreciating. The people who bought two years ago are not complaining right now.

2026 is shaping up to be a defining year for buyers AND sellers here in Southwest Florida.
