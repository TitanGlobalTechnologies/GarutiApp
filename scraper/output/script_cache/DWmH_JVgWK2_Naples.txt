Here's what Naples buyers are laser focused on in 2026 and honestly it's pretty consistent across the board.

First, full remodels. White oak cabinets, designer lighting, modern black fixtures, that moody vibe that just hits different. Buyers here want to walk in and feel like it's done.

Second, big ticket items already handled. New roof, updated AC, refreshed pool. In Naples the pool situation is non negotiable so if it's not updated buyers are already doing math in their head.

Third, location location location. Whether it's Old Naples, Moorings, or Park Shore buyers want the best street in the best neighborhood. You literally cannot move a house.

These three things are separating the listings that fly from the ones that sit.
