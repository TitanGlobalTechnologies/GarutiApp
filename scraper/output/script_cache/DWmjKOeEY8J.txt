If I had a second home in Naples this is exactly what I would do with it.

Then I would use it myself in the off season when Naples is honestly still gorgeous and way less crowded.

Here is the thing most people sleep on. Naples real estate has appreciated like crazy and inventory is still tight. So you are building serious equity while someone else covers your costs.

A second home here is not a luxury anymore. It is honestly one of the smartest moves you can make right now.
